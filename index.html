Central India's Largest Educational Institute

Founded in 1999, Technocrats Group of Institutions is one of the largest and oldest NBA accredited educational groups in central India. It comprises 11 institutes with about 17,000+ students and 1,429+ faculty members on campus. They offer undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral courses in disciplines engineering , management, pharmacy, commerce and sciences, law, as well as nursing and paramedical college. Technocrats group is the largest Technical Educational Group and the top engineering college with best placements in Bhopal.
